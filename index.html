My Process

01 - Discovery

Understanding the Challenge

I begin each project by deeply understanding the problem space, user needs, and business objectives. This phase involves stakeholder interviews, user research, and competitive analysis.

  • Stakeholder interviews and requirement gathering
  • User research and needs analysis
  • Competitive analysis and market research
02 - Definition

Defining the Solution

With a clear understanding of the challenge, I define the solution space through user journeys, information architecture, and clear success metrics.

  • User journey mapping and flow diagrams
  • Information architecture development
  • Success metrics definition
03 - Design

Crafting the Experience

I create intuitive and efficient solutions through iterative design, starting with low-fidelity wireframes and progressing to high-fidelity prototypes.

  • Wireframing and rapid prototyping
  • Visual design and UI development
  • Interactive prototyping
04 - Validation

Testing & Iteration

I validate solutions through user testing and stakeholder feedback, iterating based on insights to ensure the final product meets all objectives.

  • Usability testing and feedback collection
  • Iterative refinement
  • Documentation and handoff
