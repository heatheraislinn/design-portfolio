2025 HHN Tracker: Mobile App for Halloween Horror Nights Superfans

My goal with this project was to explore rapid prototyping with AI tools to create a mobile app for Halloween Horror Nights superfans to track their progress through the season, as well as to brush up on my skills with mobile app design.

Project Overview

Challenge

How might I leverage AI tools to develop an idea for something fun and thematic that serves a niche audience, while brushing up on my skills with mobile app design?

Approach

Collaborate with AI to refine idea, understand persona, pin down core features, and rapidly prototype design. Work with AI to refine app-specific design patterns and features.

Project Details

Duration: 2 weeks
Role: Solo Designer, Experimenter
Tools: v0.dev, Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT
Focus: Mobile App Design

Design Process

Phase 1

Initial Concept and Goal Setting

I started with a simple goal: create something fun, thematic, and useful for HHN superfans. I used AI tools to help validate the idea and gather quick research on fan behaviors, common social posts, and what people love most about the event.

  • Worked with AI to refine the idea and gather research
  • Defined target audience and user persona
  • Created list of possible features and narrowed down to core, MVP offerings
Phase 2

UX Flow Design

I mapped out a lightweight, stat-focused experience centered around a dashboard. From there, I identified the core features:

  • Tracking visits to haunted houses, scare zones, and shows
  • Drag and drop rankings
  • Viewing progress over time
  • Syncing with phone health data (for step counts)
  • Generating shareable visual for social media
Phase 3

Visual Prototyping + Build with AI

I leaned on AI to explore visual directions and UI components keeping everything app-focused, dark-themed, and in the spirit of Halloween. Once I had a base design, I loaded my codebase into Cursor and continued to iterate as I built.

  • Worked initially to use React Native components, but had Cursor help me switch to Ionic
  • Used AI for additional reseach on what makes a useful dashboard
  • Tested different prompt strategies for generating UI components

Results & Insights

Fast ideation and research generation using AI-powered tools

Ability to seamlessly pivot when considering frameworks and libraries

Reduction in time from initial concept to working MVP

Key Insights

AI as Creative Collaboration

AI tools sometimes excel at generating unexpected design and interaction concepts that human designers might not consider, serving as powerful collaboration partners.

Mobile Design Complexity

Mobile designs require careful balance between intent and usability. AI helps with exploring this balance more efficiently.

Iteration Speed

AI tools allow for more experimental approaches without significant time investment. Persoanlly, they make me unafraid to have to go back and change things as I work.

Human Curation is Essential

While AI generates great concepts, human judgment remains crucial for selecting and refining the most viable solutions. AI can't know our users and audiences the way we do.

Next Steps

I would love to get this in front of users and get feedback on the design and functionality. My next step will be to have friends in the HHN community test it out and give me feedback.
